Masala Omelette


Description

The masala omelette is a simple yet delicious egg dish that is packed with flavours and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 2 Whole Eggs
  • Half an onion
  • Half a tomato
  • A clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Dice the vegetables
  2. Add oil to a pan and start heating on a medium flame
  3. Crack and whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt
  4. Add the diced vegetables to the whisked egg and mix
  5. Add the mixture to the pan and let it stay with a mid flame
  6. Flip the omelette once a side is done.
  7. Let it cook for 3 minutes approximately.
  8. Enjoy once done
